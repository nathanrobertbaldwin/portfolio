DevQuest

DevQuest is a web-game created used React and Redux. You play as a fledgling software engineer. On your journey, you will defeat challenges (programming bugs) and collect essential equipment (like Coffee). Defeat the final boss to become a Full-Stack Engineer!

Rignite

Rignite is an eCommerce site focused on selling computer products and accessories. Rignite offers highly customized gear for the gaming enthusiast, including incredible key-caps like "The Eye of Sauron," and gaming mats like the "Ameratsu."

NateBnB

NateBnB is a vacation rental app based on AirBnB. See spots, read reviews, and make bookings to visit your favorite places all over the world.
